for researchers · reviewed-access beta

When AI makes confident writing cheap, defensible claims become the work.

A workspace where every load-bearing claim must trace to evidence, a source, or an open question — and the export shows when text outran its support. The system does not bless sentences because they sound good.

The problem

Research has a painful version of the universal AI trust problem. Drafts arrive fast; citations and evidence lag behind. Reviewers, supervisors, and integrity offices care about one thing: can this claim survive scrutiny?

The discipline

THREE ALLOWED BACKINGS
  • your own evidence;
  • a real source or citation;
  • an explicitly marked open question.
The system is not saying the science is true. It is saying whether the manuscript has outrun the record.
