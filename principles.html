THE MANIFESTO

Physics over promises.

The era of the "blank slate" AI session is over. Seitiate enforces continuity, authority, and compounding knowledge by shifting AI from a stateless utility into a permanent fixture of personal infrastructure.

01

AI Must Accumulate

Current paradigms rely on massive, monolithic API models that reset their understanding every time you clear a chat window. We believe intelligence is useless without memory. A Context Engine's primary job is to ensure that work performed today compound the efficiency of work performed tomorrow. Your AI should not start from zero every session.
02

User Sovereignty

Your creative decisions, your structural logic, and your calibrated preferences belong to you. Too many platforms ingest user behaviors to train global aggregate models, quietly seizing your intellectual property. Seitiate isolates knowledge strictly within a Sovereign Codex. You own your memory, and you own the keys to it.
03

Deterministic Governance

A system that hallucinates when handling your most critical infrastructure is unacceptable. We believe that autonomous agents must be rigorously constrained. LLMs are allowed to be probabilistic, but the Governor executing the workflow must be rigidly deterministic, enforcing immediate kill switches the moment a process deviates from your explicit intent.
04

Hardware-enforced Safety

We believe security policies are worthless without physical isolation. True AI agency requires giving the system root access, yet allowing an AI unrestricted read/write capabilities on a live machine is deeply flawed. We enforce Ephemeral Sandboxes and Network Silence by default, protecting the host system with physical barriers, not prompt instructions.
