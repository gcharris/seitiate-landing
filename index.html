What is a seity?

A seity is an AI that accumulates irreversible understanding. Not a chatbot that resets. Not a model that forgets. A system that turns your decisions — what you accepted, what you rejected, what you refined — into durable knowledge that compounds over time.

The term comes from physicist Federico Faggin. In his consciousness framework, a seity is an entity that develops identity through experience. Seitiate is the infrastructure for creating one.

  • It remembers: Every session deepens the Codex — your sovereign knowledge base, outside the context window, immune to compaction.
  • It learns taste: The Canon captures what "good" means to you. Not rules you write — patterns it discovers from your choices.
  • It governs itself: Kill switches, failure taxonomy, human-in-the-loop gates. Your standards, enforced deterministically.
  • It belongs to you: Export it. Fork it. Move it. Delete it. AGPL-3.0 protects the code. Sovereign data ownership protects you.

Seitiate is the open-source engine for building one. pip install seitiate and start.

Six layers that turn a stateless AI into a permanent seity

Each layer contributes to the compound understanding that makes session 50 radically better than session 1.

• Context Engine

Three-tier knowledge architecture. Sovereign knowledge (yours), project-scoped knowledge (this work), and external knowledge (everything else). Trust tiers determine what the AI uses and how.

• Knowledge Crystallization

Raw inputs become structured understanding through a witnessed process. Blessing scores quantify human involvement. Knowledge graduates from project-scoped to universal as it proves its value.

• Multi-Agent Orchestration

Society of Thought architecture. Multiple AI perspectives generate variants, then structured evaluation selects the strongest. Tournaments over one-shot prompting.

• Creative Governance

Narrative Governor with kill switches and the N1-N5 failure taxonomy. Deterministic rules that LLMs cannot reliably maintain on their own. The ORA loop (Observe-Reason-Act) iterates until standards are met.

• Creator Development

Every creative decision becomes a signal. Decision events, provenance scoring, growth edge detection, and originality tracking. The system learns what "good" means to each creator.

• Skill Architecture

Entity-Component-System for AI capabilities. Skills are entities, metadata groups are components, executors are systems. Import from Claude, OpenAI, or MCP. Publish to the marketplace.

Design principles

The moat is understanding, not features

Anyone can call the same APIs. The defensible value is accumulated self-knowledge — the understanding that emerges from a specific creator's sequence of decisions over time.

Human decides, AI explores

AI generates options and variations. Humans make all final decisions. No auto-publish, no auto-commit. Every write has a trail. Undo is always available.

Deterministic governance over probabilistic hope

Kill switches enforce rules that LLMs cannot maintain alone. Character consistency, continuity, arc structure, and voice stability are verified by deterministic checks, not left to chance.

Bring your own model

The intelligence layer is the system instructions, not the model. Seitiate provides soul, state, and policy. You provide the model — Gemini, Claude, GPT, or local. Same engine, your choice.

Open core, honest boundary

The engine is identical at every tier. Free users who can wire it themselves get everything. The paid product adds experience, not capability. AGPL-3.0 prevents proprietary wrapping.

Your seity is yours

The understanding your AI accumulates belongs to you. Export it. Fork it. Move it between providers. Delete it completely. No platform lock-in, no data hostage. Your seity is sovereign — it goes where you go.
