company overview

Accountable infrastructure for AI work.

AI is making output infinite and cheap. That makes two things newly scarce: trust — was a real, accountable person behind this? — and the durable person underneath, the judgment and context that take years to build.

Seitiate builds the substrate for that: memory, governance, provenance, and delegated authority that can travel across products. The premise is simple: AI-assisted work should make a person more themselves over time, not dissolve their judgment into disposable sessions.

What we build

A session should leave more than chat exhaust. It should produce useful memory, route consequential actions through review, and export records that can be checked later. The point is portable verification first, with neutral Notary counter-signature as the trust-root ceremony.

The substrate is designed for two audiences at once: people whose intellectual work deserves a trustworthy record, and builders or institutions who need to know that AI-assisted work was actually overseen.

Two proof points

Evidence Foundry — a research workspace where scholars connect sources, evidence, claims, writing, review, and export. It makes the substrate visible in a field where reproducibility is already a professional standard. Not the whole company — the people-side proof. For researchers →

Agentic Contract-CI — our own governed build pipeline: significant changes move through written contracts, independent LLM review, human-held approvals for sensitive work, and audit events. The company dogfoods the accountability layer before asking anyone else to trust it.

How it grows

Evidence Foundry starts with research because claims, evidence, and review are already part of the culture. It can grow by field branches — biosensors is one early branch — but the trunk stays the same: a portable record of what was claimed, what supported it, and what remained unresolved.

The Elixir engine foundation is the forward home of that loop: session to memory commitment to verifiable export to later retrieval. Evidence Foundry is the first external surface where people can touch the idea.

Operating posture

The near-term work is not a public launch campaign. It is a controlled path into serious use:

  • start with research groups and technical-university collaborators who can judge whether the workflow changes real scholarly practice;
  • treat sensitive institutional data as a governance problem, not a demo asset;
  • keep sovereignty practical: exportable records, portable context, and verification that does not require trusting our server forever.
Stage

A substrate being proven through real verticals, while the agents building the company are governed by the same accountability thesis.
