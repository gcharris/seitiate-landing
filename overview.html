company overview

We make AI work accountable.

AI is making output infinite and free. When output costs nothing to produce, two things become scarce and valuable: trust — was a real, accountable person behind this? — and the durable person underneath, the accumulated judgment that takes years to build.

Seitiate is built on the conviction that both can be made legible, portable, and verifiable. The company name is a causative verb: to cause to seity. The premise is that AI-assisted work should make a person more themselves over time — judgment sharpening, accumulated context compounding — and that this process needs a substrate trusted by the people doing the work and the institutions reviewing it.

What we build

That substrate is what Seitiate builds: memory, governance, provenance, and delegated authority in one layer. A session produces crystallized memory. Actions are governed at the right transitions. Outputs carry a signed ledger entry. Proofs are exportable and verifiable offline, without calling home.

The substrate is designed to be used from two sides at once — by people whose intellectual and creative work deserves a trustworthy record, and by builders and institutions who need to know that AI-assisted work was actually overseen.

Two proofs today

Evidence Foundry — a research-integrity workspace where scholars import evidence, build claims, and export a package with a root digest, reviewer decisions, and an audit trail. The substrate made visible in the domain where reproducibility is a professional standard. Not the whole company — the people-side proof. For researchers →

Agentic Contract-CI — the governed pipeline through which Seitiate itself is built: every significant change goes through a memo, a ruling gate, independent LLM review, a human-held tier for sensitive decisions approved from a phone, and a signed audit event. The company dogfoods the accountability layer. Internal proof today, not a product we sell.

How it grows

The research wedge is designed to grow as a network of field branches: Sensarra/biosensors is one early branch; student researchers at technical universities a near-term beta cohort; any domain where evidence needs a portable chain of custody is a candidate for the next branch. Beneath every branch, the substrate is the same.

The Elixir engine foundation closes the core loop end-to-end — session to signed memory to verifiable export (592 tests green, pre-deploy). Evidence Foundry is the first external surface where people can touch what that means.

Where we are

Pre-revenue. Building in public with unusual honesty about gaps:

  • No institutional pilots at scale yet — the near-term path is a technical-university beta network.
  • External Notary trust root not complete — engine-side export verifies offline; counter-signature is the next ceremony.
  • Hosted evaluation is synthetic or explicitly approved non-sensitive data until governance is ready.
Stage

Not a finished platform — a substrate being proven in real verticals, while the agents building the company are governed by the same accountability thesis.
