company overview

We make AI work accountable.

Seitiate is a trust substrate for AI agency — memory, governance, provenance, and delegated authority that people and institutions can inspect. Not another agent wrapper. Not identity for disposable bots. The point is the durable human underneath the work, and proof that the work is theirs.

The problem

AI is making output infinite and cheap. When prose is free, what becomes scarce is trust: was a real, accountable person behind this? And can their judgment accumulate — so today's work makes tomorrow's work better-shaped, portable, and defensible?

The industry is racing to give agents passports. Our bet is the opposite: agents are disposable; people are not. We build for the person whose judgment compounds over years — across tools, verticals, and institutions.

What Seitiate is

Seitiate (to seitiate — to cause seity) is infrastructure, not a single app. A seity is not a profile or a score. It is the process of a person's AI-instrumented judgment accumulating and becoming more coherent over time — captured, governed, exportable, and eventually verifiable.

Three layers, one floor:

Vertical productsFounder-owned applications where real work happens — research, creative work, OEM pilots. We host the harbor; founders own the fleet. Connected by thin contracts, never by import.
Seitiate+The commercial surface: identity, billing, credits, hosting, tenant isolation. Turns a product into a business on shared infrastructure.
NotaryThe trust layer: risk classification, certification, signed attestations, offline verification. A neutral third party can attest what a platform owner cannot attest for itself.
Seitiate engineOpen core: capture memory (Codex), enforce taste and quality (Canon, Governor), orchestrate agents, export portable proof. The loop every vertical sits on.

The loop

At the center is one turn of accountable work:

session → crystallized memory → governance at consequential transitions
→ signed, exportable record → offline verification → retrieval in a later session

Products are where the loop meets a domain. The substrate is what makes the loop portable, governable, and eventually provable to someone who does not have to trust us.

Where we are

Pre-revenue, building in public with unusual honesty about what is real:

  • Engine core loop runs end-to-end on an Elixir foundation branch (592 tests green) — integration target, not production deploy yet.
  • Managed commercial surface (Seitiate+) is deployed — auth, credits, tenant isolation — serving early verticals.
  • We govern our own agents through a live merge pipeline (independent review, human holds, audit) — internal proof of the thesis, not a product we sell today.
  • External Notary trust root and institutional pilots are not done yet. Saying so plainly is part of the product.
Stage

We are not asking you to fund a finished platform. We are building the substrate while proving it in real verticals — and dogfooding accountability on the agents that build the company itself.
